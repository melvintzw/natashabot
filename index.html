A brief introduction

We've worked with migrant construction workers before, giving legal education workshops. Many have faced problems that could have been solved easily had they known all this information. We hope to give you access to these short and easy answers almost anywhere when you want to know something about working here.

Frequently asked question

Does it inform my boss about my questions?

No, your questions are completely private and will not be shared with anyone else.

Do I have to pay anything for this service?

No, this is completely free for use.

Can I contact the Ministry of Manpower (MOM) through this service?

No. If you want to contact MOM, you can contact them directly here.

What if I cannot find what I need after chatting with Natasha?

If you need more help than Natasha can give you, please find one of the following:
  • Ministry of Manpower (MOM)
  • Migrant Workers' Centre (MWC)
  • Humanitarian Organisation for Migration Economics (HOME)
  • HealthServe
  • A qualified lawyer to give you legal advice
